Representives in Elections

About

There are many U.S. elections throughout the year, and both election information and political geography can shift with time.

This is an application designed to look up who a user’s elected representatives are locally and across the United States. Based on the user’s address, the application can find all the local, county, state, and federal official who represent the user in government.

Knowing who these representatives are and informing yourself about elections before voting is the most effective way to change how our government works.
