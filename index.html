There are many U.S. elections throughout the year, and both election information and political geography can shift with time. Click here to see upcoming elections.

This is an application designed to look up who a user’s elected representatives are locally and across the United States. Based on the user’s address, the application can find all the local, county, state, and federal official who represent the user in government. Click here to get information about your representatives

Knowing who these representatives are and informing yourself about elections before voting is the most effective way to change how our government works.
